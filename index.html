Never share sensitive personal details like credit card numbers, passwords, social security numbers, or banking information in comments or product reviews. RoopHub will never ask for such information through the website.

Avoid posting irrelevant links, promotional messages, or duplicate content across the website. This clutters the shopping experience for other customers and violates our community standards.

❌ 3. Don't Make False or Misleading Claims

Do not post fake product reviews, fabricated testimonials, or misleading information to deceive customers. All reviews should be genuine and based on actual product experience.

❌ 4. Don't Engage in Harassment or Bullying

Absolutely no harassment, hate speech, bullying, or personal attacks on other users or our team. Treat everyone with respect and maintain a civil tone in all interactions.

❌ 5. Don't Attempt to Hack or Manipulate

Never attempt to access unauthorized areas, manipulate website functions, use bots, or engage in any form of hacking. Trying to bypass security measures violates our terms and applicable laws.

❌ 6. Don't Sell Illegal or Prohibited Items

Do not attempt to sell or promote illegal products, counterfeit goods, or items prohibited by law in India. We strictly adhere to legal requirements and will remove such listings immediately.

❌ 7. Don't Copyright or Plagiarize Content

Do not copy content from other websites, use copyrighted images without permission, or plagiarize others' work. All content must be original or properly licensed and attributed.

❌ 8. Don't Make Threats or Post Violent Content

Never post threatening messages, violent content, or anything that could harm others. We have zero tolerance for threats, violence, or exploitation in any form.

❌ 9. Don't Manipulate Ratings or Create Fake Accounts

Avoid creating multiple accounts to artificially boost ratings, post fake reviews, or manipulate product visibility. Each user should maintain only one account for fair and transparent rating systems.

❌ 10. Don't Ignore Our Terms of Service

Always read and comply with RoopHub's Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and Community Guidelines. Violation of these terms may result in account suspension, content removal, or legal action as per applicable laws.
