To maintain a safe, trustworthy, and respectful community, all users are required to strictly avoid the following prohibited activities. Failure to comply can lead to permanent restrictions.

❌ 1. Don't Share Personal Information

User safety is our top priority. Never share sensitive personal details like credit card numbers, passwords, social security numbers, or banking information in comments or product reviews. Please be aware that RoopHub Official will never ask for such sensitive information through public website sections or unverified emails. Protecting your digital identity is a shared responsibility.

❌ 2. Don't Post Spam or Promotional Content

RoopHub is a platform for genuine wellness discovery. Avoid posting irrelevant external links, repetitive promotional messages, or duplicate content. This clutter ruins the shopping experience for other customers and is considered a direct violation of our community standards. Automated spamming is strictly monitored and blocked.

❌ 3. Don't Make False or Misleading Claims

Trust is the foundation of our brand. Do not post fake product reviews, fabricated testimonials, or any misleading information designed to deceive other customers. All user-generated content and reviews must be 100% genuine and based on actual product experience. Integrity ensures everyone makes the right health decisions.

❌ 4. Don't Engage in Harassment or Bullying

We maintain a "Zero Tolerance" policy for harassment, hate speech, bullying, or personal attacks on other users or our editorial team. Treat everyone with dignity and maintain a civil, helpful tone in all public interactions. We strive to keep RoopHub a positive space for wellness growth.

❌ 5. Don't Attempt to Hack or Manipulate

Security is non-negotiable. Never attempt to access unauthorized backend areas, manipulate website algorithms, use scraping bots, or engage in any form of cyber-attacks. Any attempt to bypass our security measures is a violation of our terms and will be reported to cyber-crime authorities as per applicable laws.

❌ 6. Don't Sell Illegal or Prohibited Items

RoopHub operates strictly within the legal framework of India. Do not attempt to sell or promote illegal products, counterfeit goods, or items prohibited by law. We strictly adhere to all regulatory requirements and will immediately remove such listings and report suspicious activity.

❌ 7. Don't Copyright or Plagiarize Content

Intellectual property is respected here. Do not copy content from other wellness websites, use copyrighted images without permission, or plagiarize the work of others. All user-submitted information must be original. Plagiarism devalues the platform and can lead to legal complications.

❌ 8. Don't Make Threats or Post Violent Content

RoopHub is a safe zone. Never post threatening messages, violent imagery, or content that promotes harm. We do not tolerate any form of violent rhetoric, exploitation, or dangerous behavior. Content of this nature is removed instantly and reported.

❌ 9. Don't Manipulate Ratings or Create Fake Accounts

Transparency is key to our rating system. Avoid creating multiple accounts to artificially boost product ratings, post multiple fake reviews, or manipulate search visibility. Each user is permitted only one account to maintain a fair and honest ecosystem for all wellness enthusiasts.

❌ 10. Don't Ignore Our Terms of Service

Knowledge of our rules is essential. Always read and comply with RoopHub's full Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and these Community Guidelines. Failure to follow these documented terms may result in immediate account suspension, content deletion, or legal action where necessary.

⚠️ Enforcement Notice:
